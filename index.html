What sets Vitalis apart

Immersive systems

Power routing, access tiers, and circuit puzzles with multiple solutions.

Diegetic UI

No floating markers—terminals, wrist displays, and lab panels tell the story.

Reactive AI

Adversaries learn routes, check noise sources, and manipulate doors and lights.

Consequences

Your choices alter containment outcomes and which paths are safe later.

How it plays

01 Observe

Scout cameras, vents, and keypads. Read logs. Note patrol loops.

02 Tinker

Reroute power, spoof badges, and chain vents or shutters to create openings.

03 Improvise

When plans break, use noise, light, and environment to reset the board.
