Enter Vitalis Laboratories

A research facility sealed by a cascading failure. Systems are failing—some by design. Your tools are knowledge, patience, and the building itself.

Story

  • Piece together what the lab was building—and why it was buried.
  • Choose who (or what) you set free when power is limited.

Gameplay Pillars

  • Player agency via interoperable systems
  • Stealth and evasion over raw combat
  • Persistent consequences across areas

Features

  • Diegetic map terminals and access logs
  • Non-linear route planning through ducts and service shafts
  • Sound, light, and power as tools and threats
